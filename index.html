About Me

Hello! I am Yudhik Agrawal and I am a final year research student at IIIT Hyderabad. I work with the Cognitive Science Lab under the guidance of Vinoo Alluri, on using music-induced movements to identify individual traits and also, improving song recommendation system.

Prior to that, I worked in Centre for Visual Information Technology (CVIT) with Dr. Avinash Sharma, on 3D-Human Body Reconstruction, Registration and generating Temporally coherent Sequence of Human Action. I also worked in Robotics Research Center (RRC) with Dr. K. Madhava Krishna, on avoiding Drone Collisions by Path Planning after doing 3D reconstruction of the surrounding obstacles(eg. Humans) which need not be static.

I have a keen interest in finding solutions using Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Hence, my area of research involves Computer vision using Deep Learning.

Since the beginning, I have been a coding enthusiast. Moreover, I love solving puzzles and have won several awards in quizzes and olympiads over the years. Also, I love to give hackathons and have won some at big platforms.

My proficiency skills also include management skills which have helped in leading the Programming Club of IIIT Hyderabad. I love music and in the future, if given a chance will love to have a hand on Guitar.
